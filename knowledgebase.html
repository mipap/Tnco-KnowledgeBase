Wholesale Support

Daily General Tasks and Responsibilities:


Ticket Administration:



*Reading and understanding (if possible) the reported issue in the ticket

*Checking and categorizing the ticket

*Changing the status to "Assigned" if it is in status "New"; Ticket can be in the "Work in Progress" status if assigned from other Remedy groups

*Assigning the ticket to someone from the applicable group

*Monitoring and collecting invalid tickets that represent bad communication from Asia Info's side

*Escalation of tickets created from TnDK to vendor(AI) side

*Priority change of tickets created from TnDK to vendor (AI) side


Remedy Groups Administration:



*Administration of every Remedy group represents keeping the group clean of tickets ie. assigning them to other group members. Group administration is defined within the Homepage of every group. Proposed weekly schedules are not strict as we support each others for breaks and meetings.

Avaya Support:



*Application and Billing Support team is responsible for providing the neccessary support (giving information regarding tickets and procedures, escalating tickets and ticket categorization) for business users and Operational Support team over Avaya contact centers. Proposed weekly schedule is not strict as we support each others for breaks and meetings.

Reporting:



*Vendor tickets report

*Escalation tickets report
