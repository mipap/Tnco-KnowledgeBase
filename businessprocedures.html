    Prospect Customer


    a. Customer has one email linked with several Customer IDs

    ​Solution: Keep just one Customer ID with a unique email.
    Emails on all other Customer IDs should be deleted but Customer IDs must be kept.

    ​b. Transfer ownership is not possible if it is between 2 prospect accounts

    ​Solution: Change one customer from Prospect to Active.

    ​c. If a box pops up with the following info "The customer already exists"

    ​Solution: You can often search the Veris customer as follows:
    1. Select Prospect under Customer Status
    2. Enter the Customer's name in First Name and Last Name
